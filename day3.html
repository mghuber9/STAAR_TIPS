Day 3: The Camera Turns...

The camera slowly rotates toward you.

A red light blinks once.

Then twice.

"Most students lose points even when they could figure it out."

Another message appears:

"They don’t eliminate wrong answers."

The red light stays on.

"Learn this now."

🔥 STAAR Tip #2

If you are unsure, start by eliminating answers you know are wrong. Even removing 1–2 choices greatly improves your chances.
