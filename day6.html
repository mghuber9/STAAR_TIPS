🔥 STAAR Tip #5

When a problem feels tricky, do more than one quick check. You can eliminate bad answers, plug in a choice, and ask whether the result is reasonable.
Fast combo move:
1) Cross out choices that are obviously too big, too small, or the wrong sign.
2) Plug in one of the remaining answers.
3) Pick the one that actually works.
