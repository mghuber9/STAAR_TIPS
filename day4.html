Day 4: The Panel Opens...

The wall panel slides open with a low metallic sound.

Inside, there is no hallway.

Only a screen.

The screen flickers once, then shows four answer choices.

"Sometimes the answer is hiding in plain sight."

Another line appears:

"When you do not know what to do... test the choices."

The screen glows brighter.

"Use the answers against the problem."
