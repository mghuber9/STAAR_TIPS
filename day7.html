🔥 STAAR Tip #6

Before you click an answer, stop and ask:
What is the question actually asking for?
Fast combo move:
1) Solve the math.
2) Re-read the question.
3) Make sure your answer matches what they asked for.

What is the number?

Careful: choose the value of the number, not just any number you see while solving.

✅ Correct. The number is 5 because 3(5) + 4 = 19.
